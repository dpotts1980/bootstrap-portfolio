About Me

Dan was born in Ann Arbor, Michigan but has called Chicago home for going on 18 years. He graduated from Depaul University with a degree in political science and history. He has long been interested in different political and social movements and how technology is used within them.

Dan has spent the past 15 years working for the Service Employees International Union, SEIU, in the roles of organizer, representative, trainer/recruiter, and negotiator. He has spear headed the union's initiative to engage its membership through a number of online platforms, especially in the realm of online membership.

A once self professed ludite, Dan has reinvented himself into a computer programmer as he has witnessed the power that can be achieved for everyday people. He has experience in the the following languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, Ajax, Node, and Sequel.

On top of his busy work schedule, Dan lives on Campus at The University of Chicago with his wife Erin. They work with student life and housing to provide support, activities and mentoring to approximately 75 students at the University. In thier limited free time they enjoy golfing, hiking, skiing, and travelling together. Recently married they honeymooned last summer in the Andulucia region of Spain as well as Morocco. They are planning a bycycle trip through the Loire Valley in France in the summer of 2019.
